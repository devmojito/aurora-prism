01 / PREMISE

Most boardrooms do not lack information.
They lack judgment.

Aurora Prism is built for the second problem. We work with leaders on the decisions that shape capital, leadership time & reputation — helping define the real question, build the evidence & deliver a recommendation worth committing to.

02 / WHAT WE DO Six questions we are built to answer

Each service is scoped around a specific decision.

Every engagement ends in a usable artefact, not a long report.

01 Hero service
"What belongs in India, and what shouldn't?"

GCC & Capability Centre Blueprint

For global companies evaluating India as a capability base, APC helps define what should sit in India, which model fits the enterprise, which city or talent ecosystem makes sense & what governance must be in place from day one.

Typical outputs
Capability map · Model recommendation · Location logic · Leadership profile · 12 to 24 month roadmap · Risk register · Board note
02
"Is India the right move, the right time, the right way?"

India Strategy & Market Entry

For international firms considering India, APC builds an independent view of market attractiveness, customer segments, partnership options, regulatory considerations, talent availability & sequencing.

Typical outputs
India opportunity memo · Entry model options · Competitor scan · Partner shortlist · First 180-day action plan
03
"One decision. Two weeks. A document worth circulating."

Executive Decision Briefs

A focused advisory memo for one high-stakes question. We combine desk research, expert conversations, competitor analysis & option testing to help leadership move from debate to decision.

Typical outputs
8 to 12 page executive memo · Recommendation · Risks · Open questions · Decision tree
04
"Who decides what, and how do we know it is working?"

Operating Model Design

We help companies translate ambition into structure: decision rights, governance, role clarity, capability ownership, leadership routines & escalation paths.

Typical outputs
Operating model blueprint · Governance cadence · Decision-rights map · Capability roadmap · Implementation sequence
05
"Bengaluru is not Hyderabad is not Pune. Which city is yours?"

Location & Talent Ecosystem Assessment

India is not one talent market. Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, NCR, Chennai & emerging hubs behave differently. APC evaluates talent depth, leadership availability, university ecosystem, peer presence, cost, attrition risk, policy support & strategic fit.

Typical outputs
Location scorecard · Talent risk assessment · Leadership availability map · Recommended city strategy
06
"What do the people you don't have access to actually think?"

Research & Benchmarking

For leaders who need a sharper fact base before a strategic discussion, APC delivers focused research on markets, competitors, operating models, capability trends & sector shifts.

Typical outputs
Benchmark deck · Expert synthesis · Competitor map · Opportunity scan · Board briefing

Each engagement is scoped around the decision, the depth of expert input required & the deliverable expected.
